Understanding The Fan Energy Index (FEI)

  • The Fan Energy Index (FEI) is a powerful engineering tool that allows you to pick the best fan system for your application.
  • It's a comprehensive energy metric for identifying fan systems that operate more efficiently, quietly, and reliably.
  • To get the most out of FEI, design to the latest versions of ASHRAE 90.1 and IECC. Include and hold duty point FEI on your fan schedule.
  • Use eCAPS®, Greenheck's engineer application suite, to find efficient fan systems by FEI ratings.
  • Browse our page and read our application guide to learn how FEI is calculated and its advantages in fan selection.

Advantages of Using FEI in Fan System Selection

  • Higher FEI values equate to lower energy consumption, operating costs, and carbon emissions.
  • Higher FEI fans have a forgiving selection point.
  • Higher FEI fans are usually quieter.
  • FEI quickly highlights inferior fans during submittal review.
